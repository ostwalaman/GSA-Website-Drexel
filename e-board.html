Mentor for Graduate Student Association

Associate Department Head of Graduate Studies, Civil, Architectural and Environmental Engineering

Dr. Michael Ryan serves as the Graduate Student Engagement & Leadership Liaison for the Graduate College and Associate Department Head of Graduate Studies at the College of Engineering. His dedication to enhancing graduate student life through active mentoring and leadership support reflects his commitment to academic and professional excellence. Dr. Ryan works closely with the GSA to foster a supportive environment that encourages student leadership and involvement in university governance.
