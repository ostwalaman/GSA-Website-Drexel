Dear Graduate Students,

Welcome to Drexel University, where your academic journey meets endless possibilities! The Graduate Student Association (GSA) is thrilled to have you as part of our vibrant and diverse community. About Us

The Graduate Student Association (GSA) serves as the governing body and planning board for graduate students in face-to-face quarter programs and recognized graduate student organizations at Drexel University. In collaboration with Campus Engagement within Student Life, the GSA advocates for all graduate students, including those in online and semester programs, working closely with the Graduate College and campus partners to enhance the overall graduate student experience.

Each year, the GSA Executive Board Members are nominated and elected by graduate students in the spring and serve from July 1 to June 30. The GSA, along with recognized graduate student organizations, organizes a wide range of programs and events, catering to the diverse interests of the graduate student community. These events foster connections among students, postdocs, alumni, faculty, and professional staff across various academic disciplines.

From research symposiums, academic panels, and interdisciplinary workshops to social activities like ski trips, white water rafting, and happy hours, the GSA offers something for everyone. These activities create a vibrant and inclusive environment that enriches the Drexel graduate student community and supports their academic and social growth.

Goals

  1. Bridge Communication: Act as the connecting link between graduate students, Student Life, the Graduate College, and University leadership, ensuring open dialogue and collaboration.
  2. Student Advocacy: Engage with the graduate student body to understand their needs better and actively champion their interests and concerns.
  3. Accessible Communication Channels: Establish clear and easy-to-use communication platforms where graduate students can voice their opinions, concerns, and suggestions.

  6. GSA Expansion: Encourage more graduate students to participate in GSA committees and the senate, helping to grow the organization‚Äôs reach and impact.
  7. Service Culture: Promote a spirit of service among students, encouraging them to contribute to the greater Philadelphia community through various initiatives.
